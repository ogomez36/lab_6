Distant Worlds: Music from Final Fantasy

I visited the Peacock Theater to see the Distant Worlds: Music From Final Fantasy Orchestra play. I had gone to the theater before multiple times to see other Distant Worlds Orchestras play, but this was the first time the orchestra would perform pieces from the latest game in the Final Fantasy franchise. The orchestra performed absolutely beautifully, and it easily became one of my most memorable experiences at the theater.

Final Fantasy VII Rebirth Orchestra World Tour

Later on, I visited the Peacock Theater once more to see the Final Fantasy VII Rebirth Orchestra World Tour. This Orchestra focused solely on pieces from the modern remake trilogy of the 7th entry in the franchise. Being able to hear both new and old pieces performed was an incredible experience, and made me eager to attend more Final Fantasy orchestras down the line.

The Peacock Theater at night is visually dazzling and creates an exciting atmosphere. Multitudes of people from different backgrounds come together to experience unforgettable events, made even more memorable by the ambiance created at night.
